Hershey's PB&J Kit Kat Launch — Campaign Evaluation

Solo project analyzing a full product launch campaign across Google Search, Meta (FBIG), and NBA Video. Ran data QA on simulated Dynata pre-launch survey (n=500+), built a multiple linear regression in Excel to identify ROAS drivers, and produced a Tableau dashboard for week-over-week performance tracking. Key finding: TSAR was the strongest predictor of ROAS (β=+0.071, p<0.001), while CTR was negatively correlated — high clicks did not guarantee return. Recommended shifting budget from NBA Video toward Search and scaling Social for conversion efficiency.

CRM & Customer Insights · Team · Data-Driven Marketing Decisions

Ulta Beauty — RFM Segmentation & Loyalty Program Strategy

Team analysis using real Ulta transactional data. Built a Two-Step Cluster model (log-likelihood + BIC) to identify 5 customer segments. Ran multiple linear regression per segment to find profit drivers. Identified Loyal Customers (Seg 2: avg profit $460, frequency 4.6x) and high-potential non-member customers (Seg 5) as primary targets for Ulta's revamped rewards program. Key finding: purchase frequency was the strongest profit predictor; UBR membership had a negative relationship with profit in Seg 2, suggesting discount over-reliance.

03 — Research

Published work

2023
Research on the Current Situation and Strategies of Independent Designer Brand Marketing
Advances in Economics, Management and Political Sciences (ISSN 2754-1169) · ICFTBA 2023 Conference · Sun Yat-sen University

Applied the 4P framework to analyze Alexander Wang and Ami Paris as case studies for independent designer brand marketing strategy. Identified three recurring problems across the category — over-reliance on designer identity (brand-person conflation risk), misaligned influencer partnerships reducing brand perception, and unreasonable pricing relative to production costs. Proposed solutions including virtual brand spokespersons, co-branding programs as a pricing and production strategy, and brand character building independent of the designer. Synthesized literature across 11 academic sources on fashion marketing, celebrity endorsement theory, and luxury brand strategy.
